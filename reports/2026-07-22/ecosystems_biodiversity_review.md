# ECOSYSTEMS BIODIVERSITY REVIEW
**Date:** 2026-07-22

**Severity:** CRITICAL

## Problem
Biodiversity loss due to habitat destruction, climate change, and other human activities is causing species extinction and ecosystem disruption

## Root Cause
Human activities such as deforestation, pollution, and overexploitation of resources are leading to habitat loss and degradation, disrupting the delicate balance of ecosystems

## Proposed Actions
- **Establish and expand protected areas such as national parks and wildlife reserves** — Increase protected area coverage by 20% within the next 5 years
- **Implement sustainable land-use practices and reduce deforestation** — Reduce deforestation rate by 30% within the next 3 years
