# EDUCATION CULTURE REVIEW
**Date:** 2026-06-21

**Severity:** HIGH

## Problem
Low levels of media and information literacy worldwide, leading to poor critical thinking and susceptibility to misinformation

## Root Cause
Insufficient integration of media literacy into formal education systems and lack of accessible, standardized curricula worldwide

## Proposed Actions
- **Develop and adopt a globally coordinated Media and Information Literacy (MIL) curriculum that is incorporated into primary and secondary school programs in all countries** — Increase the percentage of students achieving a certified MIL competency from current baseline to at least 70% within five years
- **Create an open‑access digital platform with multilingual MIL resources and teacher training modules, supported by UNESCO and the World Bank** — Enroll at least 10 million teachers and 30 million students on the platform within three years
