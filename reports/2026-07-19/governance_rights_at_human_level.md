# GOVERNANCE RIGHTS AT HUMAN LEVEL
**Date:** 2026-07-19

**Severity:** HIGH

## Problem
In many US communities, promised air pollution monitoring systems are not functioning or are missing, leading to inadequate data for protecting public health.

## Root Cause
Insufficient funding and coordination among federal, state, and local agencies, combined with weak enforcement of monitoring commitments.

## Proposed Actions
- **Allocate dedicated federal grant funds to install and maintain air quality sensors in underserved communities, with a requirement for annual performance audits.** — At least 90% of targeted communities have operational, calibrated sensors reporting data continuously within 12 months.
- **Create a public dashboard linking sensor data to health advisories, overseen by a joint federal‑state task force.** — Dashboard shows real‑time data for all deployed sensors and issues health alerts within 5 minutes of exceedance events.
