# ECOSYSTEMS BIODIVERSITY REVIEW
**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Severity:** CRITICAL

## Problem
Deforestation and biodiversity loss are occurring at a massive scale, with the world's rainforest potentially disappearing within 100 years

## Root Cause
Human activities such as forestry and agriculture are responsible for the majority of deforestation, driven by economic interests and lack of sustainable practices

## Proposed Actions
- **Implement and enforce sustainable land use policies, such as reforestation efforts and protected areas** — Reduce deforestation rate by 50% within the next 10 years
- **Promote and support sustainable forestry and agriculture practices, such as certification schemes and eco-labeling** — Increase the proportion of sustainably certified products in the market by 20% within the next 5 years
